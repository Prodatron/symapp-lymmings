1000-Introduction


INTRODUCTION TO Lymings

Lymings for SymbOS is a clone of the official mobile version of Lemmings, released in 2017 for Android and iOS mobile phones by Sad Puppy.

This version is a little different from the original, which was developed by David Jones and others and published by Psygnosis in 1991.

It is field based, not pixel based. Instead of assigning abilities and actions such as digging or parachuting directly to a lyming, fields are marked on the board. A lyming takes the ability or work action when it reaches the field.
